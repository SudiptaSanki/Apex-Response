# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want AegisStay to be a welcoming project for builders, designers, emergency-tech enthusiasts, students, and operators from different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume good intent
- Give feedback that helps people improve
- Keep discussions focused on the work
- Welcome beginners and first-time contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Deliberately hostile or exclusionary behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated bad-faith disruption

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or restrict participation when behavior violates this code.

## Reporting

If you see a serious issue, open a private channel with the maintainers rather than escalating publicly when possible.
